Global Sales Analysis Dashboard

Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes global sales performance, profitability, discount impact, and regional trends using the Global Superstore dataset.
The goal of the project was to transform raw business data into actionable insights through data modeling, DAX calculations, and interactive dashboards.

Tools Used

Power BI

Power Query

DAX

Data Modeling (Star Schema)

Dashboard Pages

Executive Summary

Overview of key business metrics, sales trends, top-performing products, and regions.

Monthly Analysis

Monthly and quarterly performance tracking with Year-over-Year (YoY) analysis.

Discount Analysis

Investigation of how different discount levels affect sales and profitability.

Regional & Market Performance

Comparison of sales and profit across regions, countries, and markets.

Key Metrics

Total Sales

Total Profit

Profit After Shipping

Profit Margin %

Total Orders

Profit YoY %

Key Insights

Orders without discounts generated the highest profit margins.

Discounts above 30% led to negative profitability.

Sales and profit performance varied significantly across regions and markets.

YoY analysis showed positive business growth trends.
